About This Archive

This digital archive represents a comprehensive collection of hominin fossil specimens, documenting the remarkable story of human evolution over the past 7 million years.

From the earliest potential hominins like Sahelanthropus tchadensis discovered in Chad, to anatomically modern humans found across the globe, each specimen tells a unique chapter in our shared ancestry.
