A Working Fossil Archive

This is a one-person reference project for comparing hominin fossils without having to dig through a dozen tabs every time I want the basic context for a specimen.

The archive is intentionally practical: specimen names, age ranges, discovery sites, source notes, images, and 3D models where they help. Fossil dates and classifications move as papers get revised, so the goal is to keep the records clear and easy to check.

Editorial rule If a detail is uncertain, revised, or hard to verify, it belongs in the notes instead of being polished into fake certainty.
